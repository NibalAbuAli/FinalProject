About Us

We Are Halhul Co-operative Association For High Education

Halhul is a Palestinian city located on the Jerusalem-Hebron road with a population of over 30,000 people. The Halhul Association for Higher Education was licensed on November 8th, 1955, with a registration number of 63. The association was active in achieving its goals until 1967 when its work was suspended due to the occupation. In 2004, the association resumed its activities thanks to the educated elite and philanthropists in the city. The main objective of the association is to raise the educational and social level of the city's residents. The association focuses on caring for and supporting university students, who make up almost 1,800 of the city's residents, half of whom require financial assistance. To determine the amount of aid to be provided, the association conducts an annual social survey of the students' families' conditions.

Why Choose Us

Commitment to Education

At Halhul Co-operative Association For High Education, we are dedicated to fostering educational opportunities for all. Our commitment to education is unwavering, and we strive to be a beacon of support for students seeking to pursue higher learning.

Competitive Educational Loans

We understand that financing education is a significant concern. That's why We provide interest-free student loans. Our goal is to alleviate the financial burden on students, ensuring that they can focus on their studies without excessive financial strain.

Scholarships for Excellence and Need

We believe in recognizing and nurturing talent. Through our scholarship programs, we offer financial assistance to outstanding students, empowering them to reach their full potential. Additionally, we extend our support to underprivileged students, ensuring that financial constraints do not hinder their access to quality education.

Financial Assistance in Challenging Times

Education is a journey with its share of challenges. In times of financial difficulty, we stand by our students. Our association provides extra financial assistance to those facing unexpected challenges, ensuring that they can overcome hurdles and continue their academic pursuits.

Robust Accounting and Financial System

Trust and transparency are at the core of our operations. Halhul Co-operative Association For High Education maintains a robust accounting and financial system. We adhere to the highest standards of financial management, ensuring that the resources entrusted to us are utilized efficiently and responsibly. Choose Halhul Co-operative Association For High Education for a partner in your educational journey. We are committed to making quality education accessible, affordable, and supportive for all our students.

Security and Support

Security of Financial Transactions: Our online systems and transactions prioritize security. Robust encryption methods protect your financial data. Personalized Support: A dedicated support team is available to assist with any inquiries. Personalized guidance ensures that each student receives the attention they need. Confidentiality: We uphold strict confidentiality standards to protect your personal and financial information. Your privacy is of the utmost importance to us.
